We Are DigiSchool

Providing solutions to interview preparation

At Mock prep, we want to help students prepare of technical interviews in an effective way.

As we are seeing a boom in software and related technologies, Organizations are looking for potential people to fulfill these
roles,

however interviews as seen as one of the roadblocks even by potential individuals due to lack of knowledge and guidance.

We are trying to solve this issue by working on the core basics of an interview process and allowing our users to engage with
experienced individuals in their field to be guided in the right way to overcome any gaps in their preparation.

Vision

The global job market is booming and organizations want the best people for these roles.Mock prep allows users to engage with experts in their industry who can assist them in their interview preparation by conducting mock interviews and providing required feedback

Goal

To provide a platform where job seekers can connect with experts in any field. Helping people develop interview skills and prepare better for job interviews. To help the interviewers use their skills and knowledge to earn valuable experience and income for the time they spend on the platform.

Why Us

The platform allows you to network with industry professionals and benefit from their experiences. Develop and practice interview skills by helpful feedback. Share your skills and experience with others as an experienced industry professional to obtain mentorship experience and earn money for your time.
